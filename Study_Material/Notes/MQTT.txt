1. What is MQTT?
--------------
->Wireless communication protocol.
->Works on Publish/Subscribe model.
->Used in Lightweight, Low bandwidth and Machine to Machine communication applications

2. History
--------------------
->First used in Oil and Gas insutry
->It is opensource
